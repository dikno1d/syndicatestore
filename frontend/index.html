Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy a product?

Simply click on the "Contact to Buy" button on any product card. A modal will pop up with direct buttons to reach us via WhatsApp, phone call, or Instagram. Once you select a channel, a pre-filled text with the product name and price will be generated to start our conversation.

Do I need to sign up or create an account?

No! There is absolutely no account registration required for buyers. This keeps your data private, safe, and saves you from remembering passwords.

What payment methods are supported?

We support various local payment gateways, bank transfers, mobile wallets, and QR payments. When you contact us to buy, our support team will provide you with the most convenient payment options for your region.
